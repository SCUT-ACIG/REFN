REFN Dataset Access Application

Dear Applicant, Thank you very much for your interest in the REFN dataset.

Please complete the application process below.

Important: Download & Sign License First

Before submitting your application, please download and sign the license agreement:

REFN_Dataset_License_Agreement.pdf

After completing the application form, please upload the signed PDF in the application system.

Application Process

  1. Download and read the license agreement
  2. Sign the PDF (handwritten or electronic signature)
  3. Click the button below to fill out the application form
  4. Upload the signed PDF in the application form
  5. Wait for approval (typically 3-5 business days)

Eligibility Requirements

  • Must be affiliated with a recognized academic or research institution
  • Dataset will be used for academic research purposes only
  • No commercial use or redistribution permitted
  • Must comply with all terms in the license agreement
